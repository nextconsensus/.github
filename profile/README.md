# NextConsensus

**Decision briefs for contested healthcare claims.**

NextConsensus helps institutions review medical decisions before the field
settles. We track how claims move across evidence, labels, payer policy,
guidelines, safety signals, and public dispute surfaces, then package the read
as a source-backed Review Brief.

The goal is not to predict outcomes or declare truth. The goal is to show what
changed, why it matters for a pending decision, and where a reviewer still has
usable recourse.

## Public Work

### [Refract](https://github.com/refract-org/refract)

Refract is the open-source observation engine behind part of the NextConsensus
research workflow. It reconstructs Wikipedia page-history movement as structured
evidence:

- claim and source lineage
- section and citation changes
- revert and template signals
- bounded model interpretation over deterministic evidence
